My Favorite

A series of complex folds that get buried under the centeral fold to form a table like shape. The folds hidden underneath all help hold up the structure and are essential. To me this illustrates the human condition. We are all a series of complex folds, family, personal history, and preferences that make up who we are. These folds are innately hidden on first glance, and it takes getting to know someone to slowly unfold those layers to see what makes them up. The other runner ups to my favorite both have a more visually gripping aspect to me but neither presented anything more. This fold spoke to me in a visual way but also told me a story, what more could you ask from a simple sheet of paper.
